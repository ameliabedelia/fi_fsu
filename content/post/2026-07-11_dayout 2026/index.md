For this year's day out we kept it simple and spent the day hiking near to Jena. We learned about dinosaurs on the [Sauierpfad](https://www.saurierpfad.de/) *(translation: Dinosaur Path)*, visited the Kunitzburg ruins, and enjoyed a picnic lunch on top of the [Jenzig](https://sieben-wunder-von-jena.de/home/mons-der-jenzig-berg/).
