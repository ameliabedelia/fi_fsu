We will soon be moving to our new scientific home at **[Research Center Borstel - Leibniz Lung Center](https://fz-borstel.de/en/)**. The group is no longer accepting new members in Jena. All future hires will be based in Borstel (outside Hamburg). 

***
We always welcome motivated bachelor and masters students for their thesis project. Both lab-based and computational projects are available and can be matched to your interest and background. If you are interested in doing your thesis project in the group, please send an email to **amelia.barber@uni-jena.de** with a short paragraph describing your specific interest in the group. 


We are currently recruiting the folloing roles: 

* Postdoc (Postdoktorand/in)
* Laboratory technician (Biologisch-technische/r Assistent/in)
* Bioinformatic engineer (Bioinformatik-Ingenieur/in)

Descriptions and the application portal for jobs may be found **[here](https://fz-borstel.de/en/education-training/jobs)**. *Note: job descriptions are available in English, just change the setting on the top left of the page.*

If you are a prospective PhD student or postdoc interested in applying for a fellowship or with independent funding, please get in touch with your CV and a description of your interest in the group (or potential project ideas!).
